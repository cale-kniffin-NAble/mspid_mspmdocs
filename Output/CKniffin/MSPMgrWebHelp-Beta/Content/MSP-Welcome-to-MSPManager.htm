Support

You can reach support at +1(877) 655-4689, or select the icon in the top right corner of any page in MSP Manager to access the in-app support resources.

From here, you can submit a case using the Support Portal, request and vote on new features, access the online help, or use the Live Chat option for urgent issues.

Purchasing

You can activate MSP Manager using a credit card by clicking Settings > Company Settings > Subscription, or contact our sales team at mspmanager-sales@solarwinds.com for purchasing or any other general inquiries.
