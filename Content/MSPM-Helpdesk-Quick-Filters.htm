Manage your Helpdesk Workspace with Quick Filters

Use 's Quick filters to create your own customizable quick access buttons within the ticket filter widget in your Helpdesk Workspace. Adding and configuring the filters in your Workspace Pages provides easy access to the tickets you work on the most.

Learn more about configuring Workspace Widgets

Add and configure Quick Filters

  1. After adding the Ticket Filter widget to your Workspace Page, click the Edit Workspace button in the top right of the page.
  2. Select the Edit Workspace button at the top of the Workspace page and click the Configure button in the top right corner of the Ticket Filter Widget to open the configuration window.
  3. Select Quick Filters from the options on the left of the configuration window and click Add Filter.
  4. Enter a name for the new Quick Filter and click next to Filter settings to expand it's options
  5. Enter a name for the Quick Filter and configure the filter's settings by selecting Assigned Users, Customers, Services, Contacts and Assets.

Leave the Asset field blank to include all Assets in the filter.

  1. In the Status section, select the checkbox next to each ticket status you want to include in the filter, and click the arrow to the left of each status to view the custom statuses in each main status category.
  2. Complete the remaining Filter Settings options and select the arrow next to Conditional formatting to expand it's options.
  3. Click Add Rule and select an option from the If number of items field, for instance >= for greater than or equal to, and enter a number.
  4. Choose a color option from the Format with field, or customize your own color. Continue to add Filters and Rules as needed to enhance your Quick Filters.
  5. Click at the top of the Ticket Filter Configuration editor to save the filters settings.
