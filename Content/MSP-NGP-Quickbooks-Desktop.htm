Integrate with QuickBooks Desktop and export your invoices

  Watch a video on integrating QuickBooks Desktop.

Configure QuickBooks Desktop integration with

  1. Disable the pop-up blocker in your browser.
  2. Start QuickBooks Desktop, and click Lists > Item List.
  3. In , click Settings > Company Settings > Integrations > Configure QuickBooks Desktop.
  4. Click Add a QuickBooks Desktop Item.
  5. Refer back to the Item List in QuickBooks, and add an item in for each item.

    6. The names of items in QuickBooks and must match exactly, including spacing and capitalization, to successfully export invoices to QuickBooks Desktop.

  6. In the Default Mappings area, map each line item to the defaults listed, and click Save Default Mappings.

 

Export an invoice to QuickBooks Desktop for the first time

  1. Open QuickBooks Desktop and select your company file.
  2. In , navigate to Billing, select a billing batch and click the Review & Export Invoices tab.
  3. Select QuickBooks Desktop from the Export Format list, and click Export Invoices. This adds the invoices to a queue where the Quickbooks web connector can find and export them to Quickbooks.
  4. Click the QuickBooks Web Connector link to download the QWC file.
  5. Open the downloaded QWC file and click OK to authorize to connect with QuickBooks.
  6. In the Application Certificate window, click Yes, Always Allow Access Even If QuickBooks Is Not Running, and click Continue.
  7. Click Done.
  8. In the QuickBooks Web Connector, enter your password in the MSPManagerService field.
  9. Click in a blank portion of the window to confirm the addition of your password, and click Yes to save the password.
  10. Click the MSPManagerService check box, and click Update Selected. This activates the web connector, locates the invoices waiting in the queue and exports them to Quickbooks.

Export an invoice to QuickBooks Desktop moving forward

  1. Navigate to Billing, and run a billing batch.
  2. Select QuickBooks Desktop from the Export Format list, and click Export Invoices.
  3. In the QuickBooks Web Connector, click Update Selected.
  4. Your invoices appear instantly in QuickBooks Desktop.
