FAQ

Where is the data stored?

Data is held in four data centers - two in the US, one in the Netherlands, and one 1 in Australia.

Is the data encrypted?

All data in is encrypted using Azure service fabric and thus security is dictated by the Azure security policies of those services. All the databases can also only be accessed by specific IPs or IP ranges.

Who in the organization can access the information?

All members of support, Product managers, Sales engineers, and members of management have superuser access, which allows us to log in as a tech and view system info. No one in the organization has access to passwords.

Who has the ability to reset passwords?

Passwords are encrypted with a salt and AES 256 encryption. All data is NOT encrypted at rest. Any user can reset their own password anytime.
