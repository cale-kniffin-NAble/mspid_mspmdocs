Off-site backup

Question

I back up my customer's data remotely for a flat fee of $5 per gigabyte per month. How do I charge for that?

Answer

  1. Navigate to and select a customer from the list.
  2. Select Service Items > Add Service Item.
  3. Select Flat Fee from the Service Type list.
  4. Enter 5.00 in the Unit Price field.
  5. Enter the amount of gigabytes used in the Quantity field.
  6. Select to save the Service Item.
