Add new customer information inside a ticket

You can add new customer information to open a ticket in . This will save time by adding the new customer to your account when they don't appear in the system. You can also add new locations and contacts to your account from an open ticket.

  1. Select Helpdesk > Add a new ticket.
  2. Click anywhere inside the top section of the ticket to edit ticket information.

  3. Click next to each field you want to edit.


  4. Enter all necessary information.
  5. Click Save.

saves the information added to the to your account.

When inside a ticket, hover your cursor over the ticket's Contact to view a pop up window with the name, phone number, and location for that specific contact.
