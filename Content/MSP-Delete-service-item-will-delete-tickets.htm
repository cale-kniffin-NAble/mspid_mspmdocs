Deactivate service items

You can mark any service item as inactive to remove it as an option when creating new tickets. All tickets will remain in your account and will not be deleted.

Deleting a service item will delete all tickets associated with it. Deactivating a service item keeps all associated tickets in your account.

  1. Click Customers, and select a customer in the list.
  2. Click Service Items > Edit Service Item.
  3. Clear the Active check box.
  4. Click Save Service Item.

The service item is Inactive, and will no longer be available to apply to tickets. Any existing tickets will remain in and will not be deleted.
