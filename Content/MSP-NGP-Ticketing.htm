Ticketing

has multiple ways to create and manage tickets for your customers.

What do you want to do?

  • Track your work with Helpdesk tickets
  • Use timers and quick tickets to track your time for accurate billing
  • Create multiple ticket mailboxes
  • Organize your tickets using ticket queues
  • Merge tickets and ticket requests
  • Schedule recurring tickets
  • Email customers from inside a ticket
  • Create Ticket Tasks and Task Templates
  • Use the Customer Portal to give ticket access to your customers
  • Set up ticket routing
  • Automate your helpdesk with workflow and routing rules
