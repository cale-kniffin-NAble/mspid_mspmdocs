Before configuring the integration, learn more about how communicates and connects to Xero, which kinds of data are synched between the two products and what your invoices look like after being exported from . Here are some frequently asked questions about how Xero integrates with .

How do I disconnect from Xero?

Use the Disconnect button in the top right of the Xero Configuration page in your account settings to disconnect Xero from your account.

How do invoices look after being exported to Xero? Can you edit them?

Open an invoice in your Xero account and click Invoice Options in the top right corner. Select Edit from the options in the drop-down menu to open the Invoice Editor.

While editing the invoice you can update items like Currency if you have customers in different countries, or click the Tax Rate column to select a different tax rate for certain line items in the invoice.

When and how does data sync occur? Are there any settings related to synchronizing?

Data flow between and Xero occurs when the integration is established using the Connect to Xero button in the Xero integration page in . Xero shares your file library, organization settings, business transactions and contacts with , as well as your Xero user account information.

What doesn’t your integration do?

's integration with Xero provides an easy method to track your time and services for your customers, and process your invoices when billing comes around each month. However, relies on Xero to process and handle your tax rates as well as your invoice numbering.
