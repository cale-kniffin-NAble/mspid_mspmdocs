Description

This article reviews the Unsupported Devices for the Mobile App

Environment

Mobile App

Solution

  • Currently the only specifically unsupported devices for the Mobile App are One Plus One devices and the Cyanogen OS.
  • Cyanogen is the standard OS for One Plus One devices.
  • Unfortunately Cyanogen has issues with the app. The issues are completely random, and as such are next to impossible to diagnose and reproduce.
  • The official stance from Product Management is to not support either One Plus One devices or the Cyanogen OS.
  • We do not have a timeline for a fix nor do we have any workarounds for these.
  • Windows Mobile app isn't available for now, It's in the road map but there's no estimated time of having it
