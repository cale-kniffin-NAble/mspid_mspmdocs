Can route tickets based upon the email address that is used for the mailbox?

Last Modified

Tue Oct 23 15:19 GMT 2018

Description

  • When more than one email address is used for the mailbox can tickets be routed to different users based upon the email address?

Environment

Solution

  • does not route tickets based upon the email address that is used to create a ticket.
  • NOTE: Tickets are assigned to customers based upon their email domain
  • If more than one email address is used for the mailbox they will be treated as a single entity
