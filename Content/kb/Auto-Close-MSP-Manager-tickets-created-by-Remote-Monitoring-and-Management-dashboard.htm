Auto Close tickets created by the

Last Modified

Sat May 04 13:27 GMT 2019

Description

  • is creating tickets for checks failing
  • When the issue resolves itself, the ticket isn't auto-closed

Environment

Solution

  • Within the
    • Go to the Settings tab
    • Click on PSA Integration
    • Click on Configure
    • Check Auto Close Ticket for Workstations AND Servers
    • Optional: Check Close on Check Clear
    • Click on the OK button
  • Within
    • Go to Account Settings (Cog, top right)
    • Click on Ticket Routing Rules
    • Click on When a ticket comes in via RMM...
    • Under Additional Actions section
      • For When the RMM outage(s) close and When the RMM outage(s) is cleared
      • Select Add a Note to the Ticket and Set Status to Complete
