What are the required fields when opening a ticket in ?

Last Modified

Mon Jan 20 13:49 GMT 2020

Description

  • When a portal user creates a ticket in , what fields are required to be filled in?

Environment

Solution

  • When creating a ticket via Customer Portal by clicking "Create Easy Ticket Request"
    • Required fields:
      • Email
      • Subject
      • Ticket Message
  • When creating a ticket via Customer Portal after signing in
    • Required fields:
      • Ticket Priority
      • Title
