Why does only one phone number display when hovering over a contact in tickets?

Last Modified

Wed May 27 08:55 GMT 2020

Description

  • If customer contacts have more than one phone number entered into , why does only one phone number display when hovering over contacts in tickets?

Environment

Solution

  • Contacts with multiple phone numbers only display main phone number in tickets
  • To display a different phone number in tickets, main phone number must be changed for customer contact
  1. Login to
  2. Select Select a customer on left of Customer
  3. Select customer from list
  4. Expand Customer dropdown and select Contact info
  5. Expand location and select Edit contact
  6. Enter phone number into Main phone field
  7. Select Save
