How to charge different rates according to time and location?

Last Modified

Fri Feb 02 16:58 GMT 2018

Description

  • Billing scenario for Hourly support
  • Charge different hourly rates based on the time and location of work:
    • Default: $100 per hour
    • After-hours: $150 per hour
    • Off-site: $175 per hour
  • How to charge different rates according to time and location?

Environment

Solution

  1. Click > Account Settings > Service Items.
  2. Click Add a New Service Item Template.
  3. Select Hourly from the Service Type list.
  4. Select $100 from the Default Rate list.
  5. Select $150 and $175 from the Additional Rates list.
  6. Click Create Template.

Apply this service item template to a ticket. For each time entry, choose one of the rates.
