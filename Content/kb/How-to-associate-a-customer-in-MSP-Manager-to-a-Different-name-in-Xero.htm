How to associate a customer in to a Different name in Xero

Last Modified

Wed Oct 09 10:49 GMT 2019

Description

  • Steps on how to link a customer in and Xero if they have different names.

Environment

Solution

  1. In your Dashboard
  2. Click on Customers
  3. Search the Customer you would like to link and select it
  4. On the right side of the page beside the Add Customer button click on the drop down arrow beside Actions
  5. Select Edit Customer
  6. Scroll down and look for Accounting reference
  7. Enter the name that is seen in Xero that you want to link
  8. Click on Save Customer
