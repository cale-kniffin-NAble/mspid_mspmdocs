How to setup email notification to be sent to the customer once ticket gets created and marked complete in ?

Last Modified

Tue Apr 28 09:51 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to setup email notification to be sent to the customer once ticket gets created and marked complete in ?

Environment

Solution

  1. Log into dashboard.
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Select Notifications
  4. Under Notifications, click on Notifications Settings
  5. Click on When a ticket gets created, and configure it according to your needs
  6. Click on When a ticket gets marked complete, and configure it according to your needs
  7. Click on Save Settings found at the bottom of that page to save the changes.
