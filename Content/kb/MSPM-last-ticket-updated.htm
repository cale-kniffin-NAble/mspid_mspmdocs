Description

This article describes how to check when the ticket was last updated through .

Environment

All versions

Solution

There are two ways to check when the ticket was last updated in .

On the actual ticket. Check the recent update and on the right side you will see the time stamp when it was updated.

On the Helpdesk List view. Check the recent updated time stamp on the right side for the ticket.
