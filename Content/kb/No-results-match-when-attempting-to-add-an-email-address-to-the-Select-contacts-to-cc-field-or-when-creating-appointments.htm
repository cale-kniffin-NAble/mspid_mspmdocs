"No results match" when attempting to add an email address to the "Select contacts to cc field," or when creating appointments

Last Modified

Thu May 09 20:49 GMT 2019

Description

  • "No results match" when attempting to add an email address to the "Select contacts to cc field," or when creating appointments.
  • Email address is not listed under the customer contacts.

Environment

  • Ticketing
  • Appointments

Solution

  • By design, email addresses not in the contacts list for a customer cannot entered into CC field while a ticket is being created from a ticket request.
  • Email addresses that are not under contacts list for customer can be CC'd when adding a time entry to a ticket.
