Create a block of hours that will expire after a certain time

Last Modified

Wed Oct 03 17:35 GMT 2018

Description

  • This article describes a way to create a block of hours that will expire after a certain time.

Environment

Solution

  1. Go to Account Settings > Templates > SERVICE ITEMS > Adda New Service Item > Select Service Type = Hour Retainer.
  2. Fill out the necessary fields.
  3. Under the Retainer Period Field, select "Quarterly" - every 3 months.
