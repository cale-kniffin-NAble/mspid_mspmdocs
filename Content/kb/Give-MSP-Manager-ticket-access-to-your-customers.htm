Give ticket access to your customers

Last Modified

Mon Feb 06 16:25 GMT 2017

Description

  • We would like go give our external customers access to see their tickets logged in .

Environment

Solution

  1. Click Customers, and click a customer in the list.
  2. Click Contact Info.
  3. Expand a location, and click icon next to a contact.
  4. Under Customer Portal, select one or both user roles for the contact:
  • Portal users can view their tickets and create tickets for themselves.
  • Portal admins can view and create tickets for all users within the company.
