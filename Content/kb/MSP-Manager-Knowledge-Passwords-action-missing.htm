Description

  • Knowledge Passwords action missing

Environment

Solution

  • To enable this permission follow the steps below in NGP:
    1. Click Settings
    2. Click User Management
    3. Click Roles
    4. Click Edit role for role you wish to edit
    5. Click Customer Management
    6. Select Edit customer info
    7. Click Save Role
