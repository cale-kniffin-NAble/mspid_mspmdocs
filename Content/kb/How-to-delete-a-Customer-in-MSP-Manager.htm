How to delete a Customer in ?

Last Modified

Fri Nov 22 19:44 GMT 2019

Description

  • This article explain how we can delete Customers in
  • The following steps only applies to https://app.mspmanager.com

Environment

Solution

  1. Login to and select the company from the drop down list and click Go
  2. Click Customers tab
  3. Select the Customer that you want to delete
  4. Click Actions
  5. Select Delete Customer
  6. Click Yes, I want to delete
  • Customers in can be deleted but they are then marked "Inactive" and can still be viewed under Inactive Customers for historical purposes. Customers cannot be permanently deleted due to potential risk to integration with .

Article Number: 110347

Internal notes: If customer requests that we remove the "Customers" from the back-end this is something that cannot be done. Comment from Product Management Team - This is not something supported in our product or process. Hard deleting data is a non-trivial exercise fraught with complexity and risk. Also, since there is an integration with RMM involved, there are potential trick-down implications and also a good chance the data will simply be re-propagated by the RMM sync process. So unfortunately this is not a request that we can or should entertain.Please see https://jira.solarwinds.com/browse/NCI-14306
