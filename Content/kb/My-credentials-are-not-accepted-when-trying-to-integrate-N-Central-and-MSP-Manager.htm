Description

  • My credentials are not accepted when trying to integrate and

Environment

Solution

  • As an alternative to using valid credentials in , false credentials can be created within for integration purposes only
  1. Log into
  2. Go to 'Settings' > 'Company Settings' > 'Integrations' > 'N-central' > 'Configure'
  3. Click 'Add Credentials'
  4. Enter an email address, password, and confirm password
    • Email address does not have to be accessible or even valid, but must be of the form <string>@<domain>.<topleveldomain>
  5. Click 'Save Credentials'
  6. Please make sure there's valid SSL certificate
