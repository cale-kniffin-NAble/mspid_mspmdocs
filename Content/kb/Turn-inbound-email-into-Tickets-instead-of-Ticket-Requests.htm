Turn inbound email into Tickets instead of Ticket Requests?

Last Modified

Tue Oct 29 15:03 GMT 2019

Description

  • Why does not automatically create tickets from inbound emails?

Environment

Solution

  • Inbound emails received in via the Monitored Ticket Mailbox create Ticket Requests which can be viewed in Helpdesk > Ticket Views > Ticket Requests.
  • Users can then click Create Ticket to manually turn a ticket request into an open ticket.
  • To fully automate the process, please assign an Email Domain and a Default Service Item to your customers by following the steps below:
  1. Click Customer, and select a customer in the list
  2. Click drop down Arrow beside Actions
  3. Click Edit Customer
  4. Enter the customer's email domain in the Email Domain field
  5. Click Save Changes
  6. Click Service Items
  7. Click the icon
    next to the service item
  8. Under Service Info > Status, check Default Service Item
  9. Click Save
  • From now on every Ticket Request should be automatically converted into an Unassigned Ticket.
  • To also automate the process of converting all Unassigned Tickets into Open Tickets, please assign a User to every Customer by going to Edit Customer > Assigned Users
  • For more information, see Configure ticket routing.
