Billing scenario: Managed desktops or servers

Last Modified

Wed Nov 30 21:47 GMT 2016

Description

I manage my customers' computers for a flat fee of $50 per computer per month. How do I charge for that?

Environment

Solution

  1. Click Customers, and click a customer in the list.
  2. Click Service Items.
  3. Click Add a New Service Item.
  4. Select Flat Fee from the Service Type list.
  5. Type 50.00 in the Unit Price field.
  6. Enter the number of computers in the Quantity field.
  7. Enter comments to display on invoices in the Service Item Description field. You can note a change in quantity.
  8. Click Create Service Item.
