Last Modified

Description

  • How do I change my password in ?

Environment

Solution

  1. Access https://app.mspmanager.com/login
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click Next.
  3. Below the Password field, select Forgot Password?
  4. Enter the email address associated with the account.
  5. Click Send Help
    NOTE:
    • Password reset instruction will be sent to the email address provided
    • Please make sure to check spam/junk folders
    • If you still can't access and cannot reset the password, please contact Technical Support
