Time Entry rounding issues with

Last Modified

Tue Jul 16 14:55 GMT 2019

Description

  • Time that shows 15 minutes (.25 hours) when stopping timer is being rounded up to 0.5 hours
  • Timer does not show time in seconds
  • Seconds are rounded to nearest minutes - Example: 15 minutes 35 seconds rounds to 16 minutes which rounds to nearest tenth (20 minutes), nearest quarter hour (30 minutes) nearest Half hour (30 minutes)

Environment

Solution

  • Time rounds up by design - Example: 15 minutes 35 seconds rounds to 16 minutes
  • Time does not round down - Example: 15 minutes 35 seconds rounds down to 15 minutes
  • If you do not wish to have time rounding up please disable Time Rounding:
    1. Log into
    2. Go to Settings > Company Settings > Default Settings
    3. Change Time Entry Rounding to None
    4. Click Save Settings
