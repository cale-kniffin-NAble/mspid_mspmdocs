Bill for open tickets

Last Modified

Wed Mar 20 15:31 GMT 2019

Description

  • How do I bill all tickets at the end of the month, including partial time for tickets that are still open?

Environment

Solution

  • now offers partial billing for most ticket types to allow billing for Expenses and Time Entries even if a ticket is not marked Complete.
  • When adding a Time Entry or Expense to a ticket with a compatible Service Item/Service Plan, the charge will appear in the appropriate billing period for that Service Item/Service Plan automatically
  • Partial billing is not available for Hourly Retainer or Money Retainer Service Items/Service Plans
