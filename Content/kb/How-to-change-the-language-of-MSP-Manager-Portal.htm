How to change the language of Portal

Last Modified

Sat May 09 15:30 GMT 2020

Description

  • Steps on how to change the language of Portal (x.portal.mspmanager.com)

Environment

Solution

  • To change the language of portal you may follow the steps below
  1. Login to your portal (x.portal.mspmanager.com)
  2. On the upper right corner, click on Language
  3. Select the Language that you want to apply to your portal
