Prevent associated tickets from being deleted when deleting a service item

Last Modified

Thu Mar 09 19:20 GMT 2017

Description

  • This article describes how to prevent tickets associated to a deleted service item from also being deleted.

Environment

  • All versions

Solution

  1. Create a new service item.
  2. Re-assign tickets to the new service item.
  3. Delete the service item that is no longer needed.
