unable to export to QuickBooks Online/Desktop due to special characters

Last Modified

Tue Sep 18 09:39 GMT 2018

Description

  • is unable to export to QuickBooks Online/Desktop due to special characters
  • The following error shows in QuickBooks Desktop: QuickBooks found an error when parsing the provided XML text stream

Environment

  • Quick Books

Solution

  • This error often occurs because there are non-ASCII characters in one or more tickets in a billing batch which is being exported to QuickBooks
  • The following post on QuickBooks shows more information on this:
    • https://accountants-community.intuit...ml-text-stream
  • The issue is on Quickbooks end, however there is a way to export the information to QB:
    1. Look at the billing batches you are exporting
    2. Look at all tickets assigned to the billing batches
    3. Look at the Ticket titles, Ticket Description, Time entry Descriptions and Expense descriptions
    4. Remove any Non-ASCII characters from the tickets
    5. Re-generate the billing batch that the ticket was in, and re-generate the invoice.
    6. Export the billing batches again
