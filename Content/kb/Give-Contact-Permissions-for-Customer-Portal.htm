Give Contact Permissions for Customer Portal

Last Modified

Thu May 30 15:44 GMT 2019

Description

  • How can I give a contact access to the Customer Portal to submit tickets?
  • How can I give a person Portal Admin access so they can view more tickets?

Environment

Solution

  1. Sign in to at app.mspmanager.com
  2. Click on the Customers Icon (list symbol)  in the left side bar menu.
  3. Select the Customer who the contact belongs to.
  4. Click Contacts and select the pencil icon next to the contact that requires portal access.”
  5. Expand the Location the contact belongs to.
  6. Click the edit button to edit the contact.
  7. Click the check box for Portal User/Portal Admin.
    • Portal User will allow the contact to see any tickets they have submitted.
    • Portal Admin will allow the contact to see any tickets associated with that Customer.
  8. Click Save
