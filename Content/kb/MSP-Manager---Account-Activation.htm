- Account Activation

Last Modified

Tue Jun 25 00:52 GMT 2019

Description

  • Partners may not be able to login to and they can not reset their password

Environment

Solution

  • This can occur if the account has not been activated yet.
  • When an account is first created for , an email is sent to the user to activate the account.
  • Passwords can not be reset if the account has not been activated.
  • To correct this, the admin will need to login (or Support)
  • Click Settings from the gear in the bottom left > Users & Permissions > Users
    • From here, locate the user that is having issues activating their account and click the Resend Activation Email option
      • Clicking this will resend the user the activation email and then they will be able to activate the account
  • Note: If still not activating, try activating in InCognito or Private mode to isolate environment issues.
