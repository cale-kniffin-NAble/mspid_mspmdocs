Description

This article details how to provide access to partners who have signed up for a paid or trial account.

Environment

Solution

Two emails will be provided:

  1. N-able Case Email
    • Email that informs them of their subscription which contains the link for logging in.
  2. Activation Email
    • There is also an email that is automatically generated from that contains a link to activate account with an individual invitation code.
      • Sender Address: sales@mspmanager.com
      • Subject: Welcome to - Activate Your Account

Things to check if you did not receive the activation email:

  • Check Junk mail and Spam Folder
  • Open a Case with N-able to verify if it was sent to the correct email address and resend it


NOTE: We do not have a way to activate an account without that unique activation code which is only sent via email. It can sometimes take up to 10 minutes to receive depending on the mail server. N-able can try using a different email address if no email is received.
