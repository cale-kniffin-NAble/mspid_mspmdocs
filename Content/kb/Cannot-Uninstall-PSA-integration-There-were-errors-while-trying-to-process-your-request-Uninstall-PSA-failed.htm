Cannot Uninstall PSA integration: There were errors while trying to process your request: Uninstall PSA failed.

Last Modified

Wed Sep 30 15:39 GMT 2020

Description

  • Within the :
    • Click on Settings
    • Click on PSA Integration
    • Select Uninstall and enter password
    • ERROR: 'There were errors while trying to process your request: Uninstall PSA failed' is displayed

Environment

Solution

  • The instance has been removed, while PSA integration was still intact.
  • Therefore, when the RM dashboard attempts to unlink it cannot reach locate the account
  • Gather relevant information and raise to Application Engineers
