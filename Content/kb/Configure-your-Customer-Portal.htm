Configure your Customer Portal

Last Modified

Thu Jun 27 15:04 GMT 2019

Description

  • Configure and customize your Customer Portal

Environment

Solution

  1. While logged into the console, click on the Settings in the left side bar menu
  2. Ensure the Company Settings sub-menu is open
  3. Under Company Settings on the left pane, click Portal
  4. Add the portal URL prefix, company display name, and company logo.
    • NOTE: You can set the primary and accent colors as well.
  5. Click Save Settings.
  • A contact must have Portal user access in order to log in to the portal and view their own tickets. Portal Admin access is required if the tech needs access to tickets created by all users within their company.
  • See Give Contact Permissions for Customer Portal for additional information
