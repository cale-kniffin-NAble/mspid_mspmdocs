Are time entries shown in a ticket when they are viewed through customer portal?

Last Modified

Mon Sep 09 14:48 GMT 2019

Description

  • When going to portal and viewing tickets time entries are not shown

Environment

Solution

  • There is not currently an option to view time entries for a ticket when going to the customer portal.
  • A time entry report can be run in by going to Reports > Tickets > selecting Show Time Entry Detail > Apply Filters
