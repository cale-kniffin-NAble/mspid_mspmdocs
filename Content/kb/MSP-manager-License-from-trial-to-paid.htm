Description

  • Converted from a trial account to a paid account
  • Will this create a new account for ?
  • Will information be lost from trial account to paid account?

Environment

Solution

  • Partner will not receive any new account, it will be the same account of Trial period
    • Client will not lose any of his information that was on the trial
  • To have a trial account converted into a paid one have them reach out to their Account Manager
