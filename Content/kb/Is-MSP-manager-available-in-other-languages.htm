Is available in other languages?

Last Modified

Sat May 09 13:11 GMT 2020

Description

  • Is available in other languages?

Environment

Solution

  • There are 8 different languages available in the NGP version of
    • English
    • French
    • Dutch
    • Italian
    • Portuguese
    • Spanish
    • German
    • Polish
  • To change language:
    1. Click on your user button in top right hand of screen
    2. Hover to Language
    3. Select which language you want
