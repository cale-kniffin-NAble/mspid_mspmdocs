Description

This article describes the specifics of what is required to import your already existing customers directly into from .

Environment

  • This applies to 10.1 and above.

Solution

  1. Login to and select the "Gear" in the top right of your browser:
  2. Select "Account Settings."
  3. From the left side menu, select "Integrations:"
  4. Click "Configure" button under .
  5. Select "Add N-central Credentials:"
  6. Input your SO Admin username, password, and your URL:
    Example:
  7. Save the credentials, then select "Important Customers From N-central."
  8. Choose the customers you want to import.

Customer list will be added to account in the 'Customers' tab.
