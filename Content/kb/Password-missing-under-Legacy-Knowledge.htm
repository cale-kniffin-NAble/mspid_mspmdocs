Password missing under Legacy Knowledge

Last Modified

Mon Jun 15 12:39 GMT 2020

Description

  • Cannot view the Password under Legacy Knowledge.

Environment

Solution

  • With the recent update, we have begun re-homing process of your passwords from the "Legacy" knowledge sub-menu into the Knowledge menu.
  • If Password is not showing under Knowledge, it could be a browser cache, Clear Cache and Cookies is recommended.
  • Otherwise, on Chrome, Firefox or IE, log in via InCognito or Private mode, Private Window.
