Configuring Portal Access for Contacts

Last Modified

Wed May 15 13:40 GMT 2019

Description

  • How do I give my customers access to the customer portal?
  • How to configure portal access for contacts

Environment

Solution

  • Access to helpdesk tickets through the customer portal allows customers to add new support requests and check existing tickets
  • Enable the customer portal for each contact:
  1. Click the Customers tab on the left side bar menu and select a customer in the list
  2. Click Contacts in the Customer side bar sub menu
  3. Click Add Contact or select to edit a contact
  4. Enter the contact's information and under the Customer Portal section, select one or both user roles for the contact:
  • Portal users can view their tickets and create tickets for themselves
  • Portal admins can view and create tickets for all users within the customer
  1. Select the Save (floppy disc) icon to update the contact.
  • For more information on enabling the customer portal, see this article
