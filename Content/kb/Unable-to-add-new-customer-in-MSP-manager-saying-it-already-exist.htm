Unable to add new customer in saying it already exist

Last Modified

Thu Oct 22 11:49 GMT 2020

Description

  • When adding new customer in its saying customer already exist

Environment

Solution

  • Check if the customer is already added and ensure that its not inactive
  1. Click Customer on the left pane
  2. Beside the Search box, click the filter icon and tick All Customers to show all Active and Inactive customers
  • If the customer is still not showing on the list, raise a ticket to support to further investigate.
