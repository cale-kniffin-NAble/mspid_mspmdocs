How to create service level agreements (SLA) policy

Last Modified

Fri Apr 19 12:23 GMT 2019

Description

  • Steps on how to create service level agreements (SLA) policy

Environment

  • NGP Platform

Solution

  • To add a new service level agreement (SLA) policy follow the steps below
  1. Navigate to Settings > Company Settings > SLA Policies.
  2. Select Add SLA Policy amd enter a policy name and description.
  3. In the Alert Thresholds area, expand an alert level and define the following:
  • The percentage to breach when the alert is triggered.
  • The color displayed on the associated tickets.
  • The users to receive email notifications.
  1. In the Policy Settings area, click the Default Setting and enter a time in hours for each ticket status. Repeat this step for each priority section that requires adjustments.
  2. Click Save.
