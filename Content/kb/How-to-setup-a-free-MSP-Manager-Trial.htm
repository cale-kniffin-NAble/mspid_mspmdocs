How to setup a free Trial

Last Modified

Tue Feb 11 14:22 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to start a free Trial

Environment

Solution

  1. Navigate to the Trial Page
  2. Fill all fields for First Name, Last Name, Company Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Country, and State/Province
  3. Select START YOUR FREE TRIAL!
    • An email with a link to activate the trial will be sent to email address entered into Email Address field
    • NOTE: If an account was created in the past with the same email address, an account activation email will not be sent because an expired trial/account still exists for that email address.
  4. Select link in trial activation email
  5. Setup credentials after selecting emailed link to finish trial activation
