Environment

Solution

  1. Login to with a Administrator account
  2. Click Gear Icon (Top Right)
  3. Click Account Settings
  4. Under User Management click Roles
  5. Under Actions click Edit Role or click Add A New Role
  6. Make changes
  7. Click Save Role
