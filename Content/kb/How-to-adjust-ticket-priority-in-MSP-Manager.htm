How to adjust ticket priority in ?

Last Modified

Wed Oct 31 10:41 GMT 2018

Description

  • Where can we adjust the priority settings of the tickets?

Environment

Solution

  • To adjust the Priority settings:
1. Go to Settings > SLA > SLA POLICIES
2. Select the policy you use and click the Edit button (Pencil icon)
3. Under Policy Setting, click on the Priority you want to adjust
4. Set it according to your requirements
5. Click the SAVE SLA POLICY button at the top right corner of the page
