Export to QuickBooks Online failing indicating no tax codes specified

Last Modified

Mon May 13 14:37 GMT 2019

Description

  • Invoice export in to Quickbooks Online is failing indicating that there are no tax codes specified in Quickbooks Online

Environment

  • Quickbooks Online

Solution

  • This is due to Quickbooks Online's tax rate is not properly configured yet
  • To configure it properly, please see the documentation here
  • Confirm that you have created Tax Item in Quickbooks then re-run export to Quickbooks Online
