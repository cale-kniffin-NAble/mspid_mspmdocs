Set customers as inactive

Last Modified

Wed Nov 30 21:47 GMT 2016

Description

I have a customer who no longer uses my services. How do I hide them from my active customer list, but not delete their information entirely?

Environment

Solution

  1. Click Customers, and click a customer in the list.
  2. Click Edit Customer.
  3. Click Inactive as the Status.
  4. Click Save Changes.

If this customer hires you again, set their status back to active. First, locate them in the customer list:

  1. Click Customers.
  2. Click
    Showing Active Customers, and click Inactive Customers.
  3. Click Edit Customer.
  4. Click Active as the Status.
  5. Click Save Changes.
