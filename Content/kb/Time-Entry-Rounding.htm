Time Entry Rounding

Last Modified

Fri May 01 14:24 GMT 2020

Description

  • Why extra time is being added to Time Entries?

Environment

Solution

  • Time Entries will be rounded up as per the Time Entry Rounding settings
  • You can disable or change this feature by going to Settings > Company Settings > General
  • For more information about Timer Entry Rounding and Grace Period Rounding, see the User Manual.
