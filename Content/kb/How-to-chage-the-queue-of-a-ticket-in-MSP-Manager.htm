  • To change the queue of a ticket, follow the steps below:
  1. In your , go to Helpdesk
  2. Search for the ticket that you want to change the queue
  3. Click the ticket to Edit it or use the dropdown on the righ and select Edit ticket
  4. Scroll down under Ticket Properties look for Queue icon , use the dropdown to select the queue
  5. Click the Save button
