Environment

Solution

  • To create a Portal User you may follow the steps below:
  1. Login to
  2. On the left Pane , Click Customer and Search for the customer name.
  3. Click the customer where you want to add the portal user.
  4. Click Contacts.
  5. On the upper right corner , click ADD CONTACT.
  6. Enter the contact details and tick the Portal user checkbox.
  7. Click the Save icon.

NOTE:

  • You can also edit an existing contact that you want to enable portal access.
  • Only customers contacts with portal enabled can login to portal URL. MSP Users will not have access to the portal.
  • Refer to the helplink for more information.
