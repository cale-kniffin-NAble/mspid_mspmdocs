Unable to update ticket, getting an error "Date outside range"

Last Modified

Tue Jun 30 08:04 GMT 2020

Description

  • When you enter a time entry to a ticket within there is an error stating "Date outside range"

Environment

Solution

  • This error occured when the time entry is already expired, to check this
  1. Take note of the customer name assigned to the ticket
  2. In , search and click the Customer
  3. Click on Service Items
  4. Edit the Service Item that applies to the ticket
  5. Adjust the End Date and make sure its valid
  6. Click the Save icon
