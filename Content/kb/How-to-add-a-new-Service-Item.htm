How to add a new Service Item

Last Modified

Fri May 11 14:20 GMT 2018

Description

  • How to add a new Service Item in ?

Environment

Solution

  1. Click Customers and click a customer in the list;
  2. Click Service Items;
  3. Click Add a New Service Item. Add a new service item from an existing template, or enter a new one;
  4. Enter the service, billing, rates, and invoicing information;
  5. Click Create Service Item.
