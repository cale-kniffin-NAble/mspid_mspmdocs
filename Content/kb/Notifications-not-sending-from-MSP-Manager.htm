Notifications not sending from

Last Modified

Tue Dec 01 22:47 GMT 2020

Description

  • Notifications do not send from
  • Not receiving ticket responses, appointments, and tickets assigned/completed updates

Environment

Solution

  • If you use the same email address that is used for your monitored ticket mailbox in your ticket responses or appointments (or any outbound communication), emails will not send
  • does not send outgoing emails from a monitored ticket mailbox to prevent potential endless email loop which may cause performance impact on our system
  • Solution is to not use a monitored ticket mailbox email address in any form of communication within
  • To verify which email address is used as the Monitored Ticket Mailbox:
    • Go to Settings > Tickets & Workflows > Ticket Mailboxes
