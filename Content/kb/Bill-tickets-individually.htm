Bill tickets individually

Last Modified

Wed Nov 30 21:47 GMT 2016

Description

Can I bill tickets individually when the work is completed, or does it have to be part of a billing batch?

Environment

Solution

You can bill a completed ticket at any time. Mark the ticket as complete and it will appear ready for billing under the appropriate customer in Billing.
