Body of email from Ticket Request does not get placed in ticket detail

Last Modified

Wed May 15 07:24 GMT 2019

Description

  • When a ticket request comes from an email the body of the email is not adding to the Ticket Details

Environment

Solution

  • When a ticket request comes from an email the body goes to the Response section instead of the Ticket details field
  • This is by design because Ticket Details is not rich text compatible but the Ticket Response field will often have rich text due to it containing the email body text
