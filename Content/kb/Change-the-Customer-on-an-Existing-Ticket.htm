Change the Customer on an Existing Ticket

Last Modified

Mon Jul 29 14:42 GMT 2019

Description

  • Change the customer associated with a ticket in

Environment

  • , all versions

Solution

  • Must have multiple customers in , each customer with a service item
  • Must have an existing ticket assigned to a customer
  • Tickets assigned customer can not be changed when there are time entries and information exist previously in the ticket
  1. Log in to , click on HelpDesk
  2. Select the ticket you want to modify
  3. Before you can change the customer associated with the ticket, you must delete all previous correspondence (responses, time entries, etc).
  4. After you have deleted all correspondence, select the ticket's title header to modify its basic information.
  5. Change the customer from the drop down menu, and then click Save
