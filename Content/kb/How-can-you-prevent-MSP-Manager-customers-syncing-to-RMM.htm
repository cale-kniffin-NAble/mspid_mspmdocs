How can you prevent customer's syncing to RMM?

Last Modified

Mon May 13 12:12 GMT 2019

Description

  • RMM and are integrated
  • has customers which should not be synced to RMM as they have no assets
  • How can you prevent customer's syncing to RMM?

Environment

Solution

  • For new customer's:
    1. Go into the Customers section
    2. Click Add Customer
    3. On the Customer Information page, uncheck Show on RMM Dash
    4. Enter required fields, clicking Next on each page until the customer has been created
  • For existing customer's:
    1. Go into the required Customer through the left hand menu
    2. Click the downward arrow beside Actions
    3. Click Edit Customer
    4. Scroll to the bottom and uncheck Show on RMM Dash
    5. Click Save Customer
    6. This change will Sync to RMM in around 5 minutes unless Sync Now with RMM is selected
