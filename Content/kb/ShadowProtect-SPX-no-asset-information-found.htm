ShadowProtect SPX no asset information found

Last Modified

Mon Feb 27 18:08 GMT 2017

Description

This article provides information regarding Shadowprotect SPX Monitoring service templates not applying.

Environment

  • 10.2 sp1
  • ShadowProtect SPX 6.x

Solution

In 10.2 sp1 we included ShadowProtect SPX service template to support ShadowProtect SPX monitoring.

Service Templates

  • Shadowprotect SPX Monitoring on Laptops
  • Shadowprotect SPX Monitoring on Servers
  • Shadowprotect SPX Monitoring on Workstations

If you apply the service template it throws "no asset information found".

Solution:

Without credentials, the job list cannot be retrieved/monitored. Apply credentials to the properties for the device, and then add task > update asset info. Wait for discovery to complete, then reapply the template.

Immediate workaround:

Workaround:

  1. Add the service manually on the device.
  2. Go to Device > Monitoring > click add > ShadowProtect SPX.
  3. Click on the service, then service details, and then input the credentials and job name for shadowprotect SPX.
  4. Click save.
