How to configure Ticket Routing?

Last Modified

Tue May 26 23:36 GMT 2020

Description

  • I need the steps to configure Ticket Routing in .

Environment

Solution

  1. Go to Settings in left Panel.
  2. Choose Ticket Routing > Ticket Routing Rules.
  3. Choose When a ticket comes in via email to configure ticket creation for ticket requests that come via email.
  4. Click on When a ticket comes via or When a ticket comes via RMM.
  5. Configure Settings for Ticket Routing.
