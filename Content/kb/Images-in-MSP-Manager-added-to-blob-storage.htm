Attached images in ticket responses added to Azure blob storage

Last Modified

Wed June 09 12:55 GMT 2021

Description

  • Images in email showing as broken links in .

  • Attached images in ticket responses added to Azure blob storage

  1. Send a ticket request email to @gmail.com address.
  2. Insert and Attach Images to the ticket request email and send email - wait for Ticket Request Email notification to be received at the client end.
  3. Open email notification - attached images are missing, and broken image links are displayed.
  • ISSUE: Gmail hides images by default if deemed from a suspicious sender, and notifications in are sent from noreply@mspmanager.com, which does not exist.

Environment

  • IMAP email client (gmail)

Solution

  • Gmail support article containing info on how to configure settings to always show images or ask before showing images, rather than hiding them by default:

  • https://support.google.com/mail/answer/145919?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en#:~:text=Gmail%20scans%20every%20message%20for,want%20to%20see%20the%20images
