Change the language of your account

This is different from changing the Culture Setting in your account, which only affects currency, time/date formatting and time zone.

Question

How do I change the language in my account?

Answer

  1. In the top right corner of your account, click your User Icon and select My Account.

  2. In the Personal Information tab, click the Language text box to expand it's options and select your preferred language.

  3. Click Save at the bottom of the page to confirm and save your language selection.
