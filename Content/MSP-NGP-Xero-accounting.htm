Integrate with Xero and export your invoices

  Watch the video on integrating Xero.

Configure Xero integration with

  1. Disable the pop-up blocker in your browser.
  2. In , click Settings > Integrations > Xero > Configure.
  3. Click Import from Xero.
  4. Click OK to confirm you disabled the pop-up blocker.
  5. Log in with your Xero account.
  6. On the Authorize Application window, click Authorize.
  7. Click Import from Xero.
  8. Select the inventory items you want to import into , click Import, and click Close.
  9. In the Default Mappings area, map each line item to the defaults listed.
  10. Click Save Default Mappings.

Export an invoice to Xero

  1. Click Billing, and run a billing batch.
  2. Select Xero Online from the Export Format list, and optionally select an Invoice Date and Invoice Due Date.
  3. Click Export Invoices.

Your invoices appear in Xero.
