Add tags to your assets

Create Asset Tags to order and group your assets for easy searching and filtering.

  1. Navigate to and select a customer from the list.
  2. Select Assets, select a specific asset to view it's details and select Overview.
  3. Enter your Asset Tag in the Tags section.
  4. Press Enter to apply the new Tag to that Asset.

    5. After adding a Tag, it is saved for future use in other Assets.

  5. Repeat this process to add additional Tags to the Asset.
  6. Save the Asset when finished.

Click Knowledge > Assets and use the Asset Tag filter to search for assets by their tags.
