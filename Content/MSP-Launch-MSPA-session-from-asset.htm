Start an MSP Anywhere remote session

You can remotely access your customers' devices while working on a ticket or asset. This can help you take control of these assets when you need to perform complex tasks, or access advanced features quickly. Start a remote session from three different locations in your account.

Launch remote access from a ticket

  1. Click Helpdesk > Select a ticket.
  2. Select a ticket with an attached asset, or add an asset to a ticket.
  3. Click the asset name in the ticket details section to launch MSP Anywhere.


Launch remote access from an asset

  1. Click Knowledge > Assets.
  2. Select an asset in the list that has been imported from MSP Anywhere.
  3. Click the Remote Control button at the upper right of the page to launch MSP Anywhere and start a remote session.

Launch remote access from the asset list

  1. Click Knowledge > Assets.
  2. Locate an asset in the list that has been imported from MSP Anywhere.
  3. Click in the Actions column to launch MSP Anywhere and start a remote session.
