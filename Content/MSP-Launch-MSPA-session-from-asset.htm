Start Take Control remote session to N-Central integrated assets.

For accounts integrated with N-Central, you can remotely access your customers' devices from your Asset list as well as from Workspace pages. This can help you take control of these assets when you need to perform complex tasks, or access advanced features quickly. Start a remote session from three different locations in your account.

Launch remote access from the asset list

  1. Select Assets from the left navigation pane.
  2. Locate an N-Central asset in the list .
  3. Click in the Actions column to launch Take Control and start a remote session.

Launch remote access from an Asset

 

  1. Click Knowledge > Assets.
  2. Select an asset in the list that has been imported from MSP Anywhere.
  3. Click the Remote Control button at the upper right of the page to launch MSP Anywhere and start a remote session.
