Create service items for project based billing.

Create service items for project based work and charge your customers a one-time fee for the entire project, or bill by the hour with one or more hourly rates. Generate an invoice for your customer prior to starting work, or create an invoice and collect payment after the project is completed.

Question

I design websites for customers. I provide an estimate before beginning work, but I bill hourly for time worked at the end of the job. I charge different hourly rates based on the type of work:

  • Design: $60 per hour
  • Development: $80 per hour
  • Consulting: $100 per hour

How do I configure a service item for that?

Question

I build and remodel server rooms. I charge a flat fee of $700 for 10 hours, and an hourly rate of $100 after the initial 10 hours of the project.

How do I configure a service item for that?

Answer

  1. Follow steps 1 - 4 above.
  2. Enter 700 in the Project Estimate field and 10 in the Estimated Hours field.
  3. Select Project Estimate in the Bill For field.
  4. Select $100 from the Default Rate field.
  5. Click Create Service Item.
  6. Select Project Estimate in the Bill For field.
  7. Click Create Service Item.

This immediately creates a billable amount in the Billing page for the Project Estimate amount.
