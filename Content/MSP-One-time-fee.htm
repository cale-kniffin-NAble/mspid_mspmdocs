One-time fee

Question

I sell network audits for a one-time fee of $5000, but I still want to track hours worked. How do I charge for that?

Answer

  1. Click and select a customer from the list.
  2. Click Service Items.
  3. Click Add Service Item.
  4. Select Flat Fee from the Service Type list.
  5. Enter 5000.00 in the Unit Price field.
  6. Enter 1 in the Quantity field.
  7. Under Invoice Options, ensure Create Detailed Invoices is selected.
  8. Click Add to save the Service Item.

Open a ticket for each issue to track time worked for this customer. All work will be included in the $5,000 fee, and each time entry will show $0.

You can attach detailed invoices to the customer's bill that show all time entries for work you did.
