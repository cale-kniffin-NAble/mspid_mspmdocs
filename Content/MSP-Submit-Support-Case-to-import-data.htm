Question

Can I import my customer list from other PSA or accounting tools?

Answer

The support team can help with importing your customers, contacts, locations or assets into your account.

Here's how:

  1. In your account, navigate to the Help menu and select Submit a support case.
  2. Register your account if you have not done so previously, and log in using your credentials.
  3. Enter a description of the problem (e.g. Requesting CSV import of customers).
  4. Submit the ticket.

A member of the support team will reach out will get to work importing your data and will contact you with any questions if needed.
