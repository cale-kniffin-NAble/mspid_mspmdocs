Set up your Microsoft 365 monitored mailbox

The initial configuration of your Microsoft 365 monitored ticket mailbox is a crucial step in setting up your account. Your M365 ticket mailbox enables you to receive reported issues and requests from your customers via email. Combine your ticket mailbox with workflows and routing rules and configure your ticket routing settings so that emails from your customers to are automatically converted to tickets and assigned to the correct customer and technician within your company.

Configure Permissions

Make sure all technicians who need to configure ticket mailboxes have the necessary permissions to do so.

  1. Navigate to Settings > Users & Permissions > Roles.
  2. Click next to the Role you would like to edit.
  3. Select the MSP Company Management section to expand and view it's options.
  4. Make sure the Configure Ticket Mailbox option is checked.
  5. Click Save Role to add the ability to configure ticket mailboxes to all users in that specific Role.

Configure the Microsoft 365 integration

Before configuring a M365 mailbox, make sure you have configured the integration from your account settings.

Disable the pop-up blocker in your browser before getting started.

  1. Navigate to Settings > Company Settings > Integrations and select Microsoft 365 from the Business Applications section.
  2. Click Configure and select a Microsoft Account from the pop-up window or click Use another account to enter the credentials of another Microsoft account.
  3. After the connection is established, the final page displays the connected account information and the permissions that have been configured. Click Go to ticket mailboxes to set up your Microsoft 365 monitored mailbox.

M365 mailboxes are monitored via the Graph API, which supports Multi Factor Authentication.

Set up your M365 monitored mailbox

  1. Navigate to Settings > Tickets & Workflows > Ticket Mailboxes.
  2. Click and select Add M365 Mailbox.
  3. Select the M365 email address from the list.
  4. Select Reply as to include the email address of the mailbox in the 'To' field of outgoing mail (unless overridden in individual customer settings).

    5. Only one Monitored Mailbox can have this option applied at a time.

  5. Click to save the M365 Mailbox and send a confirmation message to the specified email address.
