Run reports to track different business metrics

Use Reports to access information on tickets, customers, billed tickets, and service level agreements. After you generate a report, you can save the report as a PDF and print it.

  1. Click Reports and select a report from one of the four categories.
  2. Adjust the report filters as needed.
  3. Click Apply Filters to generate the report.
  4. Click Export and select Print to PDF, or Export to CSV.

Use the billed ticket report to provide ticket and time entry details to customers for their retainer service items.
