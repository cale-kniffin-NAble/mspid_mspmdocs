Track your work with helpdesk tickets

Manage your helpdesk with 's intuitive ticketing and time tracking system, designed for service providers with many customers.

Add a new helpdesk ticket

  1. Click Helpdesk.
  2. Click Add a New Ticket.
  3. Enter the ticket information.
  4. Click Save Ticket, or Save and Start a Timer to start tracking your time.
  5. To add additional information to a ticket, click to the right of the ticket in the Helpdesk to access a list of ticket options.

The ticket subject has a limit of 256 characters, and truncates anything over that limit.
