Off-site backup

Question

I back up my customer's data remotely for a flat fee of $5 per gigabyte per month. How do I charge for that?

Answer

  1. Click and select a customer from the list.
  2. Click Service Items.
  3. Click Add Service Item.
  4. Select Flat Fee from the Service Type list.
  5. Type 5.00 in the Unit Price field.
  6. Enter the amount of gigabytes used in the Quantity field.
  7. Click Add to save the Service Item.
