Assign customers to users

You can limit what customer information employees can access by assigning specific permissions to a technician. Deny permissions to hide those areas you do not want users to access.

  1. Click > Account Settings > Users.
  2. Click Add a new user.
  3. Complete the basic information requested.
  4. Select a Role.
  5. Use the Assigned Customers field to add customers to the user.
    1. In the Access Unassigned Customers field:
      1. Select Yes to allow access to all customers.
      2. Select No to allow access to only assigned customers.

    If you select No, employees will not be able to add customers from inside a ticket.

  1. Click Create User.

Customers are assigned to the user. You can also create roles for your employees to grant permissions for the customers assigned to them.
