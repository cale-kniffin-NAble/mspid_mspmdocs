Create and customize invoice templates in QuickBooks Desktop

Create templates to customize your invoices in QuickBooks and display the information that is most useful to your customers. Add your company logo and colors to make your invoices unique to your company.

  1. Log into QuickBooks.
  2. Select Lists > Templates.
  3. Click the Templates > New > Invoice, or double click a template in the list to edit.
  4. Select Use Logo to apply your company logo. 
  5. Select a Color Scheme and click Apply. 
  6. In Company & Transaction Information, select the items you would like included on your invoice template.
  7. Click Additional Customization.
  8. In the Header, Columns, and Footer tabs select which items to apply to the template.
    1. Select the Screen check box to view that field while editing invoices in QuickBooks.
    2. Select the Print check box to apply that field to printed invoices.
    3. In the Columns tab, enter a number in the Order column to specify the order of each column on the invoice.
    4. Click OK to save additional customizations and return to the basic customization window.
  9. Click OK to save the invoice template.

The new template is available to apply to invoices. After exporting your bills from , select your template from the top of each invoice in QuickBooks.
