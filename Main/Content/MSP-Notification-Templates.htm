Configure notification templates to send to your customers and technicians

Create notification templates to alert your customers or technicians about ticket updates, or changes to knowledge and service items. Choose from plain text or HTML notifications to send to your customers or technicians.

  1. Click > Account Settings > Templates > Notifications.
  2. Select a template from the list to expand additional options.
  3. Select External to configure the template that is sent to your customers, or Internal to configure the template sent to your technicians.
  4. Choose a language and select a format.

    5. In addition to selecting the appropriate language in the notification template editor, configure your customers' default notification options to ensure all their notifications are in the correct language: select the customer, click Actions > Edit Customer, and select their default notification language, email format, and the ticket mailbox from which notifications are sent.

  1. Click to preview the notification template.
  2. Click to send the notification.
  3. Save the template.

The notification templates are ready to send to your customers and technicians. Configure your company notification settings to define which technicians and customers receive notifications.
