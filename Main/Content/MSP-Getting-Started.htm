Add customers to your account with the New Customer Wizard

Use the New Customer Setup wizard to add customers to your account, create program levels and service item contracts, and assign locations and contacts to new customers.

Click > Quick Start, and add users, rates, service items, customers, and set up your company's support ticket mailbox.

Add customers from the Customers tab

  1. Click Customers > Add a New Customer.
  2. Add general information and click next.
  3. Select a Program Level and click next.
  4. Add custom service items, view the service items generated by your Program Level, and click Next.
  5. Add Locations and Contacts.

    6. Add assigned users to customers or locations and configure your Ticket Routing rules to automatically assign inbound tickets to them.

  6. Click Finish to view the new customer's information from the Customer Dashboard.

The new customer information, program level, service items, locations, and contacts are saved to your account.

To edit existing customers, select the customer and click Actions > Edit Customer.
